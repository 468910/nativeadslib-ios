NativeAdTableViewDelegate

open class NativeAdTableViewDelegate: NSObject, UITableViewDelegate

Wraps around a UITableViewDelegate delegate and adds the ad logic. Patching of the delegate and replaces some calls if the subject is an ad. Otherwise it’ll call the original implementation.
